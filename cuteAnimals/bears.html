Bears Fun Facts

  • Bears can live up to 50 years!
  • Bears can weigh up to 1,500 pounds! That's the weight of over 2 fridges!
  • Bears have amazing memory!
  • Although a bit sad, bears are not very social and prefer to be alone.
  • There are not any real predators to a bear (besides humans!)
