What is a Penguin?

Penguins are so dope. They are birds but they are WILD. Wild in the sense of they live in the wild (for the most part) and they also are rather large birds that don't actually fly. They live in reallllly cold places and just vibe all day tbh. They got a cute a little wobble wobble when they walk too.

Penguin Fun Facts

  • Contrary to popular belief, penguins and polar bears live in the South Pole and North Pole respectively!
  • Penguins in the South don't have any natural land predators!
  • Penguins can drink salt water!
  • Some penguin species actually live in Australia, South Africa, and Chile!
  • The 'wings' of a penguin are used for swimming, not really for flying!
