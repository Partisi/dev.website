Gaming

Playing Video Games

In my life, there really are only a few things that I have been relatively consistent with over time without getting bored. Even when I do tech or business, I do occasionally find myself drifting away from material and becoming disinterested in particular topics.

Video games for me are something that I have really always enjoyed playing to destress and relax. Since I have begun college, I did really cut off gaming from my life (as it was also kinda difficult to setup a pc in Shanghai) and now I rarely play anymore. Even though I don't play nearly as much as I used to, I do still enjoy watching youtube or twitch to pass the time while not following into a similar addiction that I had before with me playing myself.

Games I'm Interested In

Here is a short list of games that I really used to enjoy playing and occasionally watch others play to this day:

  • Call of Duty

    Game that involves shooting and is honestly just recycled over and over again each year, yet still is somewhat enjoyable. Also the game that parents think their kids are playing that causes violence in the real world.

  • Overwatch

    FPS team-based game that has a variety of different engagements to complete a certain objective. To this day, I still occasionally watch the competitive league version of this game, for its fast-paced playstyles. For those who are also familiar with Overwatch, I actually played so much that I was a top 500 main tank before I realized I was kinda addicted and quit.
